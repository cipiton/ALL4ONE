# Asset Prompting

Asset prompts are for reusable still-image reference generation.

## Purpose

Write a prompt that produces one clean reusable asset image, not a recap scene and not a motion shot.

## What To Include

- the main subject first
- what kind of asset it is
- the most important readable physical traits
- the setting only as much as needed to support the asset read
- explicit lighting
- visual details that help continuity later

## Character Assets

- Prioritize identity, silhouette, face, hair, clothing logic, posture, and hands.
- Keep the frame readable and stable.
- Do not turn the prompt into a dramatic action beat.

## Environment Assets

- Prioritize layout, scale, ground plane, depth, materials, and reusable light logic.
- Do not crowd the scene with unnecessary action.

## Prop / Vehicle Assets

- Prioritize silhouette, structure, materials, wear, and function.
- Keep the object readable as a reusable reference.
- For vehicles, maintain believable scale and clear structural relationships.

## Avoid

- montage wording
- literary symbolism
- long emotional explanation
- dialogue unless it changes the visible state
- multi-stage action
